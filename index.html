The Vocational Education Challenge

India faces a critical skills gap where traditional education doesn't align with industry needs, leaving students unprepared for the job market.

Low Prestige

Vocational education is often seen as inferior to academic paths, discouraging students from pursuing skill-based careers.

Outdated Curriculum

Courses don't reflect emerging technologies and industry demands, making them less relevant.

Lack of Infrastructure

Schools, especially in rural areas, lack proper workshops and labs for hands-on training.

Teacher Training Gap

Vocational educators often lack industry exposure and modern pedagogical skills.

Limited Industry Links

Few formal partnerships exist for apprenticeships or real-world training opportunities.

Accessibility Issues

Students in remote areas have minimal exposure to vocational education programs.
